Championship Event

Welcome to Championship Event, read below the useful information about this event.
The Championship Event event is constituted by 3 phases.

1 - Battle Classification
2 - Loser Series
3 - Final Phase

The first one, the teams will fight against each other two times. After all times have been fought, the team who have more
defeats will be eliminated, if there are more than one team with same number of defeats, the event will start Loser Series
otherwise will begin Final Phase.

The second one, the teams will fight against each other only one time. After all times have been fought, the team who have more
defeats will be eliminated, if there are more than one team with same number of defeats, the event will start Loser Series again
until there is the worst team with the most losses, then will be eliminated.

The third one, the teams survived of defeats will fight against each other only one time, the loser of each fight will be eliminated, until
the final battle with the two best server teams determine the BEST TEAM OF THE SERVER!



Rules:

Any one can register to event, to participate event the player who register is necessary have a party, be the leader party
and there isn't in command channel, case those conditions weren't respected the team participation will be cancelled
When team match his opponent, the participants will be notified that they will be teleported to Arena.
After be teleported to Arena, will be given few seconds to players get ready to battle, will be avoided
the use of Hero Items and Hero Skills. The battle only ends when an entire team is dead. After that in few seconds
all players will return where were teleport to Arena.
